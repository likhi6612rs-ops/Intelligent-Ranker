## 🚀 Overview
Recruiters often miss the right talent because traditional keyword filters ignore the nuances of experience and potential. Talent Lens is an AI-powered recruitment platform that acts as a co-pilot for hiring managers. By utilizing semantic search, vector embeddings, and LLM-driven reasoning, it evaluates candidates based on actual role fit rather than simple text matching.  
## ✨ Key Features  
Explainable Ranking: Every candidate score is backed by a "Reasoning Snippet," telling the recruiter exactly why a candidate is a top-tier match.  
Intelligent Gap Analysis: Automatically identifies skill gaps and provides probing questions for the interviewer to use during the vetting process.  
Customizable Priority Weights: Recruiters can independently adjust sliders for "Skills Match" and "Experience" without forced zero-sum balancing.
Bias Mitigation: An optional "Blind Hiring" mode that redacts personal information to ensure fair, merit-based candidate assessment.
Recruiter Toolkit: Generates custom interview questions based on the specific job description and the candidate's unique background.
